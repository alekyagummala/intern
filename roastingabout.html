  • Wit and Wordplay:

    Roasters often showcase their wit and linguistic skills through clever wordplay, puns, and creative insults. The humor is in the cleverness of the remarks rather than the severity of the insults.

  • Audience Participation:

    Roasts often involve a live audience that plays a crucial role in the comedic atmosphere. The audience reacts to the roasts with laughter, cheers, or gasps, contributing to the overall comedic experience.
